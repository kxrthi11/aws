IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service)

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is a form of cloud computing that provides virtualized computing resources over the internet. It allows businesses to rent servers, storage, and networking on a pay-as-you-go basis.

IaaS provides flexible, scalable, and cost-effective IT infrastructure that is fully managed by the service provider, allowing businesses to focus on their core activities without worrying about physical hardware management.

Benefits of IaaS

  • Cost-Effective: No need to invest in physical infrastructure.
  • Scalability: Easily scale up or down as per your requirements.
  • Accessibility: Access your infrastructure from anywhere with an internet connection.
  • Security: IaaS providers offer advanced security measures to protect your data.
